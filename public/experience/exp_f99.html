  • "It was Skip." -- Dee Weiss.  "It was not Matt." -- Bob Weiss.

    • The debate continues...  I didn't see it.  I did hear the load boom of a well-struck volleyball slamming into an unsuspecting victim.  I also heard the audible grimaces of about 15 men who were watching the play.  And, I turned around to see Joe Potts on all fours clutching his groin area in obvious pain.  Then, Joe got up to argue with the ref about some call (was Joe called for a double hit, perhaps?) and then return to the ground after losing the argument.  I don't know who it was... but if I were Matt or Skip, I'd consider wearing a cup (just in case).

Fall 2024 League Information

  • Registration for the Fall 2024 season is now CLOSED!
  • Season Dates:
    • Mon Jul 29: Registration ends ($85 all players thru this date; $95 afterward)
    • Aug 8, Aug 15, Aug 22: Preseason/tryouts (the first tryouts are focused mostly on NEW players)
    • Aug 29: NO PLAY (division drafts take place during this time)
    • Sep 5 thru Oct 10: Regular season
    • Oct 17, Oct 24, Nov 7: Playoffs! (no matches on Halloween!)

  • Format: Our league is a recreational and social co-rec volleyball league for players of all skill levels. Players sign up individually and teams are drafted by captains, which helps to form competitive teams in each division. Players rotate in and out (no subbing occurs) to encourage fair and equal playing time for all. Teams often socialize together after matches, so it can be a great way to meet new people!

  • Location: All league matches will be played on Thursday nights at the Adventist HealthCare Fieldhouse on the grounds of the Maryland Soccerplex in Germantown MD.
